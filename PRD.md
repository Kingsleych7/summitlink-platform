Product Requirements Document (PRD) v1.0

Vision

Summitlink Telecom LTD is building Africa's next-generation digital infrastructure platform by combining telecommunications, fintech, CPaaS, and value-added digital services into one secure, scalable ecosystem.

Our mission is to empower individuals, startups, enterprises, and developers with reliable communication, payment, and digital service infrastructure.

Connecting You. Empowering Possibilities.
